Interactive Resume

Ever thought regular resumes were boring? Well cause they are. It's just
a bunch of lines of text that we use to prove value in workforce.

Why not instead we do something better? How about a resume that people
can actually USE and INTERACT with? That helps show value much more.

It's the phrase "A picture can tell a thousand words" all over again, except
it's now "A HTML site with CSS and JS can tell an entire resume."
